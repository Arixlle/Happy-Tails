Meet the wonderful pets
of Happy Tails

Welcome to Happy Tails! Our mission is to provide a safe and loving temporary home for animals in need, while we work to find them their forever homes. Our dedicated staff and volunteers provide exceptional care for every animal that comes through our doors. Whether you're looking to adopt a new furry friend, volunteer your time, or donate to support our cause, we invite you to explore our website and learn more about the important work we do. Together, we can make a difference in the lives of these precious animals.
