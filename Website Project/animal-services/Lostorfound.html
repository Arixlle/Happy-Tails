Animal Services

Lost or Found Pets


Dogs and cats can run away from home, it happens more often than you think!
Happy tails is here to help and assist in finding your dog.
Additionally, if you found a dog that you think may belong to someone please keep reading.

  • Lost Pets

    If your are a pet owner trying to find your lost pet there are several things you can do:

    1. Give us a call at (123)456-7890 with a description of your pet so we can try to locate it

    2. Visit our animal shelter at 123 San Jose State during operational hours to see if we have your furry friend

    3. Visit our adoption page on our website to see if your dog is in our database

    Tips to Finding Lost Pets:

    - Dont panic! Many pets go months before being reunited with their owner

    - You can print out lost animal fliers and post it where the pet was last seen

  • Found Pets

    Happy Tails is currently accepting sick or injured pets.

    If a lost pet is found there are several things you can do to help get the lost pet back home:

    1. Bring the lost pet in to our shelter during operational hours.

    2. If you cannot move the animal or think that doing so could put you in danger, contact us at (123)456-7890 so that we can come and take the animal back home.

    Other things to note:

    - To increase the chances of reuniting the lost pet with its owner, we will take several measures including photographing and recording all stray animals, adding their details to our database, showcasing them on our website, and checking for a microchip.

    - After taking the stray pet you found to the shelter for processing, you have the option to take care of the animal in your own home.

    - Additionally, posting flyers in your neighborhood or the location where you found the pet could also be beneficial.

    - If you found an injured stray animal after our operational hours, please take it to a 24/7 vet clinic.
