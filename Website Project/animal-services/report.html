Animal Services

Animal Issue


If you see something concerning, please report it
We want to make sure animals are being treated fairly.

  • Please email us at Happytails@gmail.com if you need to report:


    - A dead domestic animal

    - A dead wild animal

    - A non-urgent humane condition

    - An over limit of animals

    - A domestic animal allowed to run at at large

    - Unsanitary conditions

    - A lost animal

    - A loose dog currently at large

  • Please call (123)456-7890 during operation hours if you need to report:


    - An aggressive stray dog

    - An injured stray or wild animal

    - A sick stray or wild animal

    - A confined stray or wild animal

    - An animal bite to a human

    - A noisy animal
